Platform Features

Everything You Need, In One Place

Avyan Pragati is the unified digital gateway bridging central & state government services, civic reporting, and citizen participation

Unified Government Portal

One platform connects every central ministry, department, and scheme — no more hunting across dozens of separate websites.

My Region Dashboard

Personalized state & district map, local government links, land records, police portals, and region-specific services — all in one place.

Central Government Services

Direct access to 12 service categories — Identity Documents, Health, Education, Housing, Employment, Finance, Transport, Agriculture, and more.

All 36 State & UT Portals

State government portals, police citizen services, grievance portals, and land record systems for every state and Union Territory.

Report & Track Civic Issues

File geo-tagged complaints with video evidence, track resolution status in real-time, and verify contractor work with a transparent timeline.

Personalized Schemes Feed

Government schemes, subsidies, and programs curated to your profile, location, age group, and interests — updated in real time.

Civic Engagement Hub

Vote in government polls, submit policy suggestions, join national discussions, and participate in MyGov-style campaigns and tasks.

Multilingual & Accessible

Full Hindi and English support across every section. Designed for accessibility on mobile, tablet, and desktop for every citizen.
